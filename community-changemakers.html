Create Lasting Community Impact

The Community Changemakers program supports young leaders in developing and implementing projects that address pressing social challenges in their communities.

Over six months, participants receive training, mentorship, and seed funding to turn their ideas into reality, while building practical leadership skills.

Program Benefits:

  • Up to $2,000 seed funding
  • Expert mentorship
  • Project management training
  • Impact measurement tools
  • Networking opportunities
