Our Kenyan Journey

Since launching our Kenyan operations in 2020, we've been committed to developing leaders who can address the unique challenges and opportunities facing Kenya today.

Our programs are tailored to the Kenyan context, focusing on:

  • Ethical leadership in public service
  • Youth empowerment and employment
  • Community-driven development
  • Gender equality in leadership
  • Sustainable business practices

Our Nairobi Headquarters

Located in the heart of Nairobi, our Kenyan office serves as the hub for all our East African operations, with satellite programs in Mombasa, Kisumu, and Nakuru.

Our Kenyan Events Timeline

Highlights of our key events and milestones in Kenya since 2020.

2024 Leadership Summit

March 15-17, 2024 | Nairobi

Our largest gathering yet with 300+ leaders from across Kenya discussing "Ethical Leadership for Sustainable Development".

Coastal Leadership Forum

November 2023 | Mombasa

First regional forum focusing on leadership challenges unique to Kenya's coastal region, with special focus on tourism and blue economy.

Youth Innovation Challenge

August 2023 | Nationwide

Competition for young Kenyan entrepreneurs with 50 finalists receiving mentorship and seed funding.

Women Leaders Conference

March 2023 | Nairobi

Annual gathering of 150 women leaders from politics, business and civil society to share experiences and strategies.

County Leadership Program

2022-2023 | 5 Counties

Expanded our reach to county governments with specialized training for local administrators and officials.

Kenya Launch Event

June 2020 | Nairobi

Official launch of Importance Leadership Kenya with keynote by prominent Kenyan leaders.
